========================================================================
    Sonar Scramble
========================================================================

This is the start of a simple submarine warfare game inspiried by Squidi
and his 300 game mechanics #85 - Sonar Battle 
[http://www.squidi.net/three/entry.php?id=85]

This is a work in progress and will be evolving as progress is made. It
may stay faithful to the original concept by Squidi or it may change if
the desire to head in a different direction takes my fancy. Only time
will tell...
